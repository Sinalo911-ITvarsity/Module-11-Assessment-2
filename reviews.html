Restaurant Reviews

Hooters

Hooters offers a lively, sports-bar atmosphere that feels like the ultimate hangout spot. The orange and white décor, combined with flat-screen TVs streaming live games, creates a fun and casual environment perfect for catching up with friends or cheering for your favorite team.

The service is friendly and welcoming, with staff who ensure you feel at home from the moment you walk in. Their cheerful demeanor and attentive approach make the experience enjoyable and memorable.

The food is the highlight, especially the world-famous wings. Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, they come tossed in a variety of sauces, from the tangy "Daytona Beach" to the fiery "Triple Dog Dare." These wings are a must-try for any visit. While the burgers, like the "Twisted Texas Melt," are packed with flavor, they don’t quite outshine the wings. Pair your meal with sides like curly fries or onion rings to complete the experience.

For drinks, Hooters has a solid lineup of options, including ice-cold beer on tap, refreshing soft drinks, and a variety of cocktails. A pint of beer paired with wings is a go-to choice for many visitors.

Prices are reasonable, making it a great spot for casual outings without breaking the bank. Most meals average around $15–$20 per person, depending on your appetite.

Overall Experience: Hooters is all about great food, friendly service, and a fun vibe. It’s the perfect spot for wing lovers, sports enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a laid-back meal with friends.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟 (4/5)

Best For: Wing cravings, casual hangouts, and sports-fueled afternoons.

Perere

Perere offers a cozy and contemporary atmosphere that’s perfect for both casual meals and special occasions. With its rustic decor and soft lighting, the restaurant creates an inviting and relaxed setting. The staff is friendly, attentive, and quick to recommend dishes, adding a personal touch to your dining experience. The menu at Perere features a mix of local and international flavors, each dish made with high-quality ingredients.

Starters: The “Perere Spicy Wings” are a must-try. Crispy and coated in a smoky sauce with just the right amount of heat, they’re the perfect start to your meal.

Main Course: The “Grilled Steak with Herb Butter” is tender and flavorful, served with roasted vegetables and mashed potatoes for a satisfying dish.

Vegetarian Option: The “Vegetable Risotto” is creamy, fresh, and a great choice for those looking for a hearty vegetarian option.

Dessert: The “Chocolate Lava Cake” is a sweet indulgence, with a molten center that makes for the perfect finish.

The drink menu offers a variety of cocktails, wines, and non-alcoholic options. The “Mango Mojito” is a refreshing pairing with any dish.

Pricing: The prices are on the higher end, with meals typically costing $25–$40 per person, but the quality justifies the cost.

Overall Experience: Perere offers a memorable dining experience with great food, excellent service, and a cozy ambiance. Whether it’s a casual outing or a celebration, it’s a solid choice for a delicious meal.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 (5/5)

Best For: Flavorful meals, cozy dining, and trying new dishes.

Tiger's Milk

Tiger’s Milk stands out as a trendy hotspot, offering a unique blend of industrial-chic decor and a lively, urban vibe. Whether you're looking for a casual dinner or a fun night out with friends, this restaurant delivers a vibrant atmosphere perfect for social gatherings.

Ambiance: The first thing you’ll notice is the edgy, urban design—think exposed brick walls, metal fixtures, and warm ambient lighting. The open-plan layout and bustling energy make it a great spot for groups, though it might feel a bit overwhelming for those seeking a quieter, more intimate setting. The outdoor seating area is a nice touch for sunny days or relaxed evenings.

Menu Highlights: Tiger's Milk boasts an eclectic menu that caters to a variety of tastes.

Gourmet Burgers: A must-try, these burgers are generously sized and packed with flavor. The “Triple Cheese Deluxe” stands out, featuring a perfect balance of melted cheeses, caramelized onions, and a juicy beef patty.

Wood-Fired Pizzas: Their pizzas are crafted with a thin, crispy crust and topped with fresh, quality ingredients. The “Spicy Chorizo” pizza delivers a satisfying kick with its bold flavors.

Craft Beer Selection: Known for their local and international craft beers, Tiger’s Milk is a paradise for beer enthusiasts. The drinks menu also includes creative cocktails and wine options to pair perfectly with your meal.

Food and Service: While the food is generally flavorful and portions are generous, the consistency of service leaves room for improvement. During peak hours, the waitstaff can seem overwhelmed, leading to longer wait times and occasional mix-ups. However, the staff’s friendly demeanor often compensates for these hiccups.

Pricing: Tiger’s Milk offers reasonable pricing for the quality of food and experience, with meals typically ranging from $15 to $30 per person. This makes it a great option for casual diners and groups looking for a flavorful yet affordable outing.

Overall Experience: Tiger’s Milk delivers a bold dining experience with its trendy vibe, diverse menu, and lively atmosphere. While it’s not without its flaws, such as inconsistent service during busy times, it remains a solid choice for those seeking a fun, energetic setting and delicious comfort food.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟½ (3.5/5)

Best For: Groups, casual diners, and those craving bold flavors in a vibrant, social environment.

Mustang Sally's Pub And Diner

Atmosphere: The atmosphere at Mustang Sally's Pub And Diner tries to capture a fun, retro vibe with its diner-inspired décor, but it feels a bit dated and lacking in charm. While the neon signs and checkered floors evoke an old-school feel, the space could use some refreshment. The lighting is harsh at times, making it feel more utilitarian than cozy.

Service: While the staff is generally friendly, the service leaves much to be desired. There are occasional long waits, and some orders are either delayed or incorrect. The staff seems understaffed during busy times, which might contribute to the slower service. While friendly, the lack of attention to detail could be frustrating for guests.

Food: Mustang Sally's Pub And Diner offers standard diner fare, but unfortunately, it doesn't stand out in terms of taste or quality.

Starters: The appetizers, such as jalapeño poppers and mozzarella sticks, are very basic. They taste like something you might find frozen and pre-packaged, rather than freshly prepared.

Main Courses: The “Sally's Signature Burger” is disappointing. The patty, while cooked well, lacks seasoning, and the toppings don’t offer anything particularly exciting. The pulled pork sandwich is also quite underwhelming—dry and lacking in flavor.

Vegetarian Options: The vegetarian burger is mediocre at best, with a texture that feels more like processed ingredients than fresh, flavorful vegetables. The salads feel uninspired, with basic ingredients and little effort in their preparation.

Desserts: The milkshakes are the highlight of the meal, but they are often too thick and difficult to enjoy without a spoon. While they are a sweet treat, they are not enough to redeem the rest of the meal.

Drinks: The drink menu is fairly standard, offering a few craft beers and sodas, but nothing that stands out. The “Mustang Mojito” was overly sweet and lacked the balance of flavors you would expect in a well-made cocktail.

Overall Experience: While Mustang Sally's Pub And Diner could be a nostalgic spot for those who enjoy diner food, it falls short in terms of both food quality and service. The meals feel rushed and lack attention to detail, and the overall experience doesn’t match the potential of the restaurant’s retro theme.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟 (2/5)

Best For: Casual outings with low expectations, or for those who prefer a quick meal without a fuss.

Soweto Creamery

Atmosphere: Soweto Creamery has a cozy, vibrant interior blending traditional African elements with modern design. It’s a relaxed spot, perfect for casual meals and dessert outings with family and friends.

Service: The service is friendly and attentive. Staff members are welcoming and quick to assist, making for a pleasant dining experience.

Food: The highlight is the ice cream, with unique flavors like “Maize and Milk” and “Pineapple and Ginger.” The “Soweto-style samoosas” and “Grilled Chicken with Pap” are great starters and mains, while the “Malva Pudding Sundae” is a must-try dessert. Vegetarian options like the “Vegetable Bunny Chow” are flavorful too.

Drinks: They offer refreshing drinks like “Hibiscus Juice” and “Lemonade with Mint,” along with coffee milkshakes for a sweet touch.

Overall Experience: Soweto Creamery is a great place for dessert lovers, with a mix of savory and sweet dishes that showcase unique South African flavors. Friendly service and a relaxed vibe make it a solid choice for casual dining.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 (5/5)

Best For: Dessert lovers and casual dining.

The Diner

The decor is charming, with vintage-style booths and bright neon lights, creating a welcoming atmosphere. It’s the perfect spot for those craving classic comfort food like juicy burgers, crispy fries, and indulgent milkshakes.

If you’re in the mood for a laid-back meal with familiar comfort food, The Diner is a solid choice, though it may not stand out if you're seeking more adventurous flavors.

Burger Box Roadhouse Diner

Burger Box Roadhouse Diner offers a great spot for those craving classic comfort food in a relaxed and casual setting. With its charming roadhouse vibe, the diner brings a welcoming atmosphere where the focus is squarely on hearty meals. The menu is dominated by burgers, which come in a variety of options—from classic cheeseburgers to more inventive creations like the "BBQ Bacon Burger" or the "Spicy Jalapeño Burger." Each burger is packed with flavor, served on soft buns, and accompanied by a generous side of crispy fries or onion rings. The patties are juicy and well-seasoned, making each bite satisfying.

The interior has a rustic charm, featuring wooden tables, vintage signs, and dim lighting, giving it the perfect roadhouse feel. It’s a no-fuss environment that’s ideal for casual meals with friends or family. The staff is friendly and approachable, though the service can be a bit slow during peak hours when the diner is bustling with customers.

While the burgers are the highlight, there are plenty of other options for sides, including crispy onion rings, creamy coleslaw, and hearty chili cheese fries. These sides complement the main dishes well, adding variety and depth to the meal. The drink menu is straightforward, offering sodas, milkshakes, and local beers—perfect for pairing with your burger.

What sets Burger Box Roadhouse Diner apart is its ability to deliver comfort food in a fun, laid-back environment. It’s not trying to be fancy, but it excels in offering classic diner food that hits the spot every time. The prices are reasonable, and it’s a great choice for a no-hassle meal, whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or enjoying a long, casual lunch with friends.

Though the service can be inconsistent at times, it doesn’t take away from the overall experience. The food is worth the wait, and the relaxed atmosphere makes it a place you’ll want to return to.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟 (4/5)

Best For: Burger lovers, casual outings, and comfort food with a roadhouse vibe.

Bellgables Country Restaurant

Bellgables Country Restaurant offers a serene dining experience surrounded by scenic countryside views, but unfortunately, it doesn't quite live up to expectations. While the rustic ambiance and peaceful setting may appeal to those looking for a quiet escape, the overall dining experience falls short.

The menu features a mix of South African and international dishes, but the food lacks the quality one might expect from a restaurant of this type. The “Sunday Roast,” though presented nicely, was underwhelming. The meat was overcooked and lacked flavor, while the vegetables seemed bland and uninspired. The “Grilled Veg Salad” was a safe, but forgettable, option for those seeking something lighter.

Service at Bellgables was slow, with the staff seeming disorganized and unable to keep up with the crowd, despite not being overly busy. This lack of attention to detail left a less-than-ideal impression.

Desserts, like the “Chocolate Mousse,” while decent, didn’t do enough to redeem the meal, and the wine selection was limited compared to other local restaurants.

While Bellgables offers a peaceful retreat, the food quality and service leave much to be desired. The prices are also on the higher side for the experience provided, making it harder to recommend as a go-to dining spot.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟 (2/5)

Best For: A quiet setting, but not ideal for food lovers.

Pier Restaurant | V and A Waterfront

Pier Restaurant at the V and A Waterfront offers a stunning setting with breathtaking views of the ocean, making it a popular spot for tourists and locals alike. The ambiance is sophisticated, with large windows allowing diners to take in the scenic beauty, especially during sunset. However, while the setting impresses, the overall experience leaves much to be desired.

The menu at Pier Restaurant showcases an assortment of seafood and fine dining options, but the food doesn’t quite match the level of excellence one might expect given the location and price point. The “Grilled Kingklip” was cooked well but lacked the vibrant flavors that the dish promised. The “Oysters” were fresh, but the accompanying mignonette sauce was too overpowering, masking the natural taste of the shellfish.

Service was polite but slow, and the staff seemed a bit disorganized, which detracted from the overall experience. For a fine dining restaurant, the attention to detail should be more consistent.

Desserts were the highlight, with the “Lemon Meringue Tart” offering a refreshing end to the meal, although portions could have been larger for the price.

Overall, Pier Restaurant offers a fantastic view and decent food, but at a premium price, the dining experience doesn’t quite live up to the expectations set by its reputation and location.

Final Rating: 🌟🌟🌟 (3/5)

Best For: Scenic views and special occasions, but not the best choice for food enthusiasts.

Sakhumzi Restaurant

Sakhumzi Restaurant in Soweto falls short of expectations. The service was slow and uncoordinated, leaving us frustrated. Unfortunately, the restaurant’s traditional South African offerings didn’t live up to their potential. We won't be returning anytime soon.
